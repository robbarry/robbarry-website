This privacy policy explains how Toaster SMS collects, uses and shares information related to its text-message program.

## Information we collect

When you use Toaster SMS, we may collect:

- Your mobile phone number.
- Your SMS opt-in status, including the date, time and source of consent.
- Text-message content you send to or receive from Toaster SMS.
- Message delivery data such as timestamps, carrier information, delivery status and error logs.
- Basic contact or account information you provide when requesting support or using the service.

## How we use information

We use this information to:

- Send Toaster SMS messages you requested or agreed to receive.
- Provide service updates, notifications and support.
- Respond to requests, questions and troubleshooting reports.
- Maintain message delivery records, monitor service performance and prevent abuse.
- Comply with applicable laws, regulations and carrier requirements.

## How we share information

We may share information with service providers that help operate Toaster SMS, such as messaging providers, hosting providers and technical support vendors. These providers are allowed to use the information only to provide services for Toaster SMS.

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process or carrier compliance requirements.

Mobile information and SMS opt-in consent will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

## Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to operate Toaster SMS, meet compliance obligations, resolve disputes and maintain business records.
