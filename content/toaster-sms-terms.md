These terms apply to the Toaster SMS text-message program.

## Program name

Toaster SMS

## Program description

Toaster SMS sends text messages related to Toaster service activity, notifications, responses and support. Messages may include service updates, account-related notices and replies to user requests.

## Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your use of Toaster SMS and the notifications or responses you request.

## Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply. Charges are determined by your mobile carrier and your mobile plan.

## Opt-out instructions

You can opt out of Toaster SMS messages at any time by replying **STOP**. After you send **STOP**, you may receive one final confirmation message and will no longer receive Toaster SMS messages unless you opt in again.

## Help instructions

For help, reply **HELP** to any Toaster SMS message or contact [toaster@robbarry.org](mailto:toaster@robbarry.org).

## Support contact

For support, contact [toaster@robbarry.org](mailto:toaster@robbarry.org).
