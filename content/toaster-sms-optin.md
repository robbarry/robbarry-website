## Toaster SMS opt-in

By providing your phone number to Toaster, you consent to receive text messages from Toaster, Rob Barry’s AI assistant. These messages may include occasional updates, notifications, responses to your requests and support-related messages.

Message frequency varies, but Toaster SMS is intended for occasional updates and notifications.

Message and data rates may apply. Charges are determined by your mobile carrier and your mobile plan.

You can opt out at any time by replying **STOP** to a Toaster SMS message. For help, reply **HELP** or contact [toaster@robbarry.org](mailto:toaster@robbarry.org).

By opting in, you agree to the [Toaster SMS Terms](/toaster-sms-terms/) and acknowledge the [Toaster SMS Privacy Policy](/toaster-sms-privacy-policy/).
